BackStory:

In september if 1974 a Japanese folk band named the Red Bird disbanded due to members having creative diffrences. How every later on fomer members decided try a gain and formed a band called Hifi Set. This group consisted of Junko Yamamoto(vocalist and guitar player), Toshihiko Yamamoto(guitar Player) and Shigeru Okawa(bass player).

Their first Album:

On February 5, 1975 They released thier debute called Graduation Photo. The album was a collection of 12 songs. The album was likly named after their song Graduation Photo, This song was written by Yumi Arai who is a Japanese singer and composer.
