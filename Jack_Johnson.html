BackStory:

Before JacK Johnson became a Musician he was a Surfer. He learned to surf at the age of 5 and but the age of 17 he was he was the youngest competitor to compete in the Pipeline Masters surfing event. Sadly 1 week later he as injured in a surfing accident. He had to have 100 stitches on his forehead and had to have teeth removed. This accident ended his surfing career. While his career ended he stills surfs to day. Here is a like to him surfing in one of his music Videos. Music video

When did he become a Musician:

After his Injure at the Banzai Pipeline he had to take some time to recover. After recovery he would attend the University of California for for Film studies. while attending this university he and his his friend would produce a surfing documentary named Thicker Than Water. While making the documentary he wrote a acoustic sound track that was used in he film. After the documentary was published the soundtrack caused the attention of a musician and their producer. They helped him launch his first album called Brushfire Fairytales in 2001 . This album contained 13 songs but if you were in japan or the uk you got bonus tracks.
