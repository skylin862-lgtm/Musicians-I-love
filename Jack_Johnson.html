BackStory:

Before JacK Johnson became a Musician he was a Surfer. He learned to surf at the age of 5 and but the age of 17 he was he was the youngest competitor to compete in the Pipeline Masters surfing event. Sadly 1 week later he as injured in a surfing accident. He had to have 100 stitches on his forehead and had to have teeth removed. This accident ended his surfing career.
